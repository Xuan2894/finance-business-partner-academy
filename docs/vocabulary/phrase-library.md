## 8. My Story Phrases

### Standard Costing

By embedding myself in the factory for a month, I was able to establish a standardized costing structure across six production cost centers.

With the standard costing system in place, commercial and operations teams could share a common view of cost drivers.

---

### Fixed Cost Absorption

The analysis showed that the increase in unit cost was primarily caused by lower production volume rather than higher spending.

---

### Gross Margin Bridge

The bridge provided management with a transparent view of margin movements and their underlying drivers.

---

### CEO Cost Gap Analysis

I focused the analysis on the largest cost drivers to maximize impact within a limited timeframe.

---

### Reporting Automation

By automating the reporting process, I was able to significantly reduce preparation time and improve reporting efficiency.
