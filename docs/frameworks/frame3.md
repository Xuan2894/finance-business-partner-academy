# Frame 3 – Enablement

## Structure

With X in place, we could Y.

---

## Why It Works

Senior leaders care about capability creation.

This frame focuses on what became possible after an initiative was implemented.

It shifts the conversation from activity to business enablement.

---

## Examples

With the new reporting system in place, we could monitor business performance on a daily basis.

With standardized costing rules in place, we could improve pricing consistency across customers.

With better demand forecasting in place, we could optimize inventory levels and reduce working capital.

---

## My Story

With detailed production routing and machine time captured in the ERP system, we could gain full visibility into factory capacity.

With this transparency in place, commercial and operational teams could distinguish whether cost variances were driven by technical processes or capacity utilization.

As a result, the business could make more informed pricing decisions and better protect margins during quotation discussions.
