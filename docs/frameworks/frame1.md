# Frame 1 – Result → Driver

## Structure

X was driven by Y
"[Result] was driven by [driver]."
"[Result] was primarily attributable to [driver]."
"[Result] reflected [driver]."
---

## Examples

Revenue growth was driven by stronger customer demand.

Margin improvement was driven by a more favorable product mix.

Cost savings were driven by process optimization initiatives.

---

## Personal Example

Gross margin improvement was driven by better visibility into production cost drivers.

## Claude

"Fixed overhead was underabsorbed, driven by a shortfall in production volume against the budgeted level."

"Pre-GP margin contracted by 5pp, driven by an unfavourable shift in product mix toward lower-margin items."

"Capacity utilisation fell below the budgeted level, driven by poor alignment between planned and actual production schedules — resulting in the underabsorption of fixed overhead costs across the affected cost centres."
