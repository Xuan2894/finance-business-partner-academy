# Common Communication Mistakes

## Pattern 1

Concept → Explanation → Example → Impact

Example:

The system helps people understand the cost.

---

## Better

Impact → Reason → Evidence

Example:

The new costing structure enabled teams to identify key cost drivers and make more informed pricing decisions.

# Common Communication Mistakes

## Pattern 1

Concept → Explanation → Example → Impact

Example:
The system helps people understand the cost.

---

## Better

Impact → Reason → Evidence

Example:
The new costing structure enabled teams to identify key 
cost drivers and make more informed pricing decisions.

---

## Pattern 2 — Word Choice Mistakes (Session: Frame 2 & 3)

### Mistake 1: Wrong verb — "reduce hours"
❌ "I was able to reduce 8 hours per team member."
✅ "I was able to save 8 hours per team member."

Rule: You **save** hours. You **reduce** time or cost.

---

### Mistake 2: Wrong word — "unmatched"
❌ "The unmatched concept between accounting and operations."
✅ "The misalignment between accounting and operations."

Rule: "Unmatched" sounds positive (no equal). 
Use **"misalignment"** when two things do not fit together.

---

### Mistake 3: Passive result — "received approval"
❌ "I received approval from the CEO."
✅ "I secured approval from the CEO."

Rule: **"Secured"** shows you drove the outcome actively.
**"Received"** sounds passive — like it happened to you.

---

### Mistake 4: Informal verb — "dealing with"
❌ "Instead of dealing with the commercial team."
✅ "Instead of resolving disputes with the commercial team."

Rule: **"Dealing with"** is too casual for executive communication.
Use **"resolving disputes"**, **"negotiating with"**, or **"aligning with"**.

---

### Mistake 5: Wrong noun — "analyst"
❌ "The team could focus on analyst."
✅ "The team could focus on analysis."

Rule: **"Analyst"** = a person. **"Analysis"** = the work.

---

### Mistake 6: Non-existent word — "boardly"
❌ "The boardly dashboard."
✅ "The board-level dashboard."

Rule: Always say **"board-level"** as a compound adjective.
