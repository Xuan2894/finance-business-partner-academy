# Executive Communication Principles

## Principle 1

Reality before Concept

---

Weak:

We improved operational efficiency.

Strong:

Machine utilization increased from 72% to 85%, improving operational efficiency.

---

## Principle 2

Evidence before Conclusion

---

Weak:

The pricing strategy was ineffective.

Strong:

Win rates declined from 35% to 22%, indicating the pricing strategy was ineffective.

---

## Principle 3

Impact Landing

Never stop at activity.

Always finish with business impact.

---

Weak:

I developed a costing system.

Strong:

I developed a costing system, improving cost visibility and supporting more accurate pricing decisions.

---

## Principle 4

Executive Brevity

One idea.

One sentence.

One impact.
