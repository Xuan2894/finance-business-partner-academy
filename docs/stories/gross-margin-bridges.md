# Story 3 – Gross Margin Bridge

## Situation

Management observed fluctuations in gross margin but lacked a structured explanation of the underlying drivers.

Different departments often had conflicting interpretations of performance.

---

## Task

I was responsible for creating a clear bridge between standard gross margin and actual settled gross margin.

---

## Actions

I developed a gross margin bridge that isolated the impact of:

* Volume
* Price
* Product Mix
* Material Cost
* Manufacturing Efficiency
* Settlement Adjustments

I worked with multiple functions to validate each component and ensure consistency.

---

## Result

Management gained a transparent understanding of margin movements.

The bridge became a common framework for performance reviews and improved the quality of business discussions.

Decision-making became faster because stakeholders focused on drivers rather than debating numbers.

---

## Key Interview Message

I transform financial data into business insights.
