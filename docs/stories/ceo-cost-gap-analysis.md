# Story 4 – CEO Cost Gap Analysis

## Situation

The CEO requested an urgent analysis to explain why internal yarn costs were significantly higher than market prices.

The analysis was needed within three hours.

---

## Task

I had to identify the major cost gaps, validate assumptions, and provide management with actionable insights under severe time pressure.

---

## Actions

I gathered information from sourcing, costing, and production teams.

I broke the gap into individual components including:

* Raw material cost
* Waste rates
* Processing cost
* Capacity utilization
* Overhead allocation

I prioritized the largest drivers and focused the analysis on areas with the highest financial impact.

---

## Result

The analysis was completed within the deadline and provided management with a clear understanding of the major cost drivers.

The discussion shifted from assumptions to evidence-based decision-making.

Management was able to focus on the most significant improvement opportunities.

---

## Key Interview Message

I can provide high-quality financial insight under pressure.
