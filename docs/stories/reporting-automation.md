# Story 5 – Automated Reporting System

## Situation

Monthly reporting required significant manual effort and consumed valuable finance resources.

The process was slow and prone to human error.

---

## Task

I wanted to automate the reporting process and improve reporting efficiency.

---

## Actions

I redesigned the reporting workflow using:

* Excel Power Query
* Power BI
* Power Automate

I standardized data sources, automated refresh processes, and created dynamic dashboards for management reporting.

---

## Result

Reporting preparation time was reduced from several days to a matter of minutes.

Management gained faster access to business information and the finance team could spend more time on analysis rather than data preparation.

---

## Key Interview Message

I use automation to increase the strategic value of finance.
